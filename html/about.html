About the Project

SPVMS is Bangladesh's flagship national initiative to build a world-class, technology-driven public transport management system. This platform brings smart city transport solutions tailored for Bangladesh's unique urban landscape.

Our Vision

To create a unified, intelligent transport network that connects every corner of Bangladesh — from bustling Dhaka streets to remote Sylhet hills — making public transport safe, efficient, affordable, and environmentally sustainable for all 170 million citizens by 2030.

Strategic Goals
  • Achieve 95% on-time performance across all modes
  • Integrate 100% of formal transport vehicles digitally
  • Provide real-time info to 170 million Bangladeshis
  • Create 50,000 smart transport management jobs
  • Establish Bangladesh as South Asia's transport tech leader
  • Reduce transport-related carbon emissions by 35%
