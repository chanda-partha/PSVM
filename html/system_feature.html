System Features
 comprehensive suite of intelligent tools designed to revolutionize Bangladesh's public
         transport infrastructure with cutting-edge technology.

Real-time Vehicle Tracking

GPS-powered live tracking of buses, metro, CNGs, and ride-share vehicles across all 64 districts with sub-second updates.

Smart Route Management

Intelligent route optimization with dynamic rerouting, traffic analysis, and multi-modal journey planning for passengers.

Smart Alert System

Proactive incident management with automated notifications to operators, drivers, and passengers during disruptions.

Live Transport Monitoring

24/7 centralized monitoring dashboard with anomaly detection, vehicle health alerts, and operator performance metrics.

Fare Management System

Unified digital ticketing across all transport modes with contactless payment, mobile wallets, and student discounts.

Vehicle Profile Management

Every vehicle is assigned a unique ID with a dedicated monitoring profile — tracking certifications, fine history, and compliance status in one place.

All Transport Modes— One Platform

From city buses to metro rail, ride-sharing to auto-rickshaws — every transport mode 
            integrated into one unified smart management platform.
